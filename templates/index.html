Loot Hunt

Welcome to Loot Hunt! This AR Game is insipired by video game easter eggs and hidden package achievements.

A Loot is a geo-cache of fake internet points hidden around major events in Ontario. A Hunt is the group of Loots for a particular event or location.

To learn about the game and how to play check out our help page or get involved on social media.

If there are active or upcoming Hunts you can check them here. We hope to expand Hunts outside of Ontario in the future. Although most points are found on Hunts, a hint or Bonus Drop codes could be found on social media!
